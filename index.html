BAC Exam Format

Understanding the official exam structure

P1

Reading Comprehension

1.5 hours / 14 points

  • • One passage (500-700 words)
  • • Comprehension questions (open-ended)
  • • Vocabulary/Word meaning questions
  • • Title or main idea questions
  • • Multiple choice or true/false
P2

Written Expression

1.5 hours / 6 points

  • • Choose ONE from 3-4 options
  • • Argumentative essay (250-300 words)
  • • Narrative or descriptive essay
  • • Opinion/Personal essay
  • • Letter writing (sometimes)

Total Score: 20 Points

Our goal: Help you achieve 14+ out of 20
